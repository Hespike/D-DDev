Characters

In D&D there are two types of Characters. In the first group there are the PCs or Playable Characters, they are created and controlled by the Players. The second group consists of NPCs or Non-Playable Characters, these are the actors in your story, controlled by your Dungeon Master. You can find characters for both of these groups.
