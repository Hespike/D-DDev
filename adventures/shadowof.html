Shadow of the Black Winged Lord

All miracles last for three days. This one did as well. The legendary flying fortress, Griseus stopped over the small, insignificant town of Stevren two weeks ago, but by now, it is just part of the scenery. Its mysterious residents haven't been seen yet, and no one can reach the fort. Legends say the fort is inhabited by seven great dragons, along with the last Tisterii survivors of Heavens War. But the fort is silent, abandoned. Over the horizon, the large dust cloud can mean only one thing. The Imperial Legion is on the march, and they are closing in on Stevren.

Running this adventure

The Shadow of the Black Winged Lord is an adventure for four to five 10th-level adventurers. The adventure starts in the small farming community of Stevren, in the shadow of Griseus, the flying fortress from an ancient war. The characters will have a choice, if they want to side with the Tristarii holed up inside Griseus, and take part in their long plan spanning a hundred thousand years, or join the side of the legion to take down this threat to the imperial peace. Regardless of the choice, the adventure has a series of encounters, both social and combat focused, and skill challanges. These vary in difficulty, some can be navigated with relative ease, others being challanging even for a ready and prepared party. Make sure your players know that some encounters are not easily winnable, and will need their best performance, such as choosing to fight the tristarii and the dragons, or the imperial battlemages.

Author(s): Sárdi András, Mészáros Zoltán Péter and Hajdu Tamás
