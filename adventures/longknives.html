Time of the Long Knives

Assassins running rampant!

The Commonwealth is in chaos! Lords, officials and messengers are dropping like flies. Slit throats, poisoned goblets, disappearances marked the past month. But who gains enough though the chaos to make these horrors worthwhile? And why did these people lurking in the shadows take an interest in a group of adventurers?

Running this adventure

This adventure is designed for three to five player character with an avarage party level of 3. The story takes place in a large city on the brink of chaos, and focuses on subterfuge, intrigue and investigation. Open combat will often not be the easiest solution. Player characters with a wide array of skills, social aptitude, and spells focused on manipulation will excel in the adventure, but tanky martial characters will get their time to shine as well. The players will have to find who to trust and help, and who to double-cross and fight.

Author(s): Sárdi András, Mészáros Zoltán Péter and Hajdu Tamás
