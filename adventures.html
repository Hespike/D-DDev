Home-made Adventure Modules

Shadow of the Black Winged Lord

All miracles last for three days. This one as well. The legendary flying fortress stopped over the town of Stevren two weeks ago, but by now, it is just part of the scenery. Its misterious residents havn't been seen, and no one can reach the fort. But the horizon carries the dust cloud of the legion marching. The peaceful town may not worry about the mountain floating above them, but the Arcanominium has their own interests.

Era of the Long Knives

The Commonwealth is in chaos! Lords, officials and messengers are dropping like flies. Slit throats, poisoned goblets, disappearances marked the past month. But who gains enough though the chaos to make these horrors worthwhile? And why did these people lurking in the shadows take an interest in a group of adventurers?

Battle for the Approval Ratings

The title of the best empolyee ever is in danger of being lost to the pretender Zolt 'An. The great hero of creation, Thomas must make kind of an effort to put the usurper back in his place. And we need (not really) brave adventurers to help the hero achieve his goal.


Yepos Goldclock

„Poltragowtól északra, és Longbowtól észak-nyugatra található a Mérleg nyelve, az Égmetsző járatain keresztülhaladva északra. Az Égmetszőben megtalálod a kulcsot, amelyen keresztül nézve megláthatod az alagút végén rejlő igazságot, az igazságot, amely kiegyenlíti a kontinens viszonyait végre, megtisztítja azt, te pedig elnyered jutalmad érte.” Vajon mi várja kalandorainkat ezen a rejtélyes helyen. Vajon mi történt Yepos Goldclock-kal?
